# INWX

Set the record's **Provider** to **INWX**, then fill these fields.

- **Hostname**: the host. INWX derives the record from your credentials, but enter the host you manage.
- **Login**: your INWX username. A dedicated DynDNS account is recommended.
- **Password**: your INWX password.

_Ported from ddclient's `nic_inwx_update`._
